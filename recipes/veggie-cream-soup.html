Veggie Cream Soup

Eat your vegetables to grow strong enough to defend Hyrule!

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups mixed vegetables, like carrots, broccoli, zucchini, bell peppers.
  • 1 onion, diced.
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced.
  • 2 cups vegetable broth.
  • 1 cup heavy cream or coconut milk.
  • Olive oil.
  • Salt and pepper to taste.
  • Fresh herbs for a garnish.

Instructions:

  • In a pot, heat olive oil and saut√© onions, garlic, and mixed vegetables until slightly softened.
  • Add the broth and bring the mixture to a simmer until the vegetables are fully cooked.
  • Blend the soup until smooth, then return to the pot and stir in the cream or coconut milk. Heat through without boiling and season with salt and pepper.
  • Serve hot, garnished with fresh herbs.

Description:

A creamy, dreamy soup bursting with the flavors of fresh vegetables. Its smooth texture and delightful taste are sure to please veggie-lovers and comfort food enthusiasts alike.
